public class SafeBitmapShader
extends android.graphics.BitmapShader
The SafeBitmapShader class is designed to work in conjunction with SafeTranslatedCanvas to work around various Android issues with large canvases. For the two classes to work together, call onDrawCycleStart(com.mapbox.mapboxsdk.views.safecanvas.ISafeCanvas) at the start of the com.mapbox.mapboxsdk.overlay.OverlayOverlay#drawSafe method of your com.mapbox.mapboxsdk.overlay.OverlayOverlay. This will set the adjustment needed to draw your BitmapShader safely on the canvas without any drawing distortion at high zoom levels and without any scrolling issues.
